# ClipCraft

**Edit photos, GIFs and videos directly on Telegram — no apps, no websites.**

Send a file, pick what to do, get the result in seconds.

👉 **[Open on Telegram](https://t.me/clipscraftbot)**

---

## What it does

**Photos**
Resize, compress, convert (JPG / PNG / WEBP), crop and remove the background automatically.

**GIFs**
Resize, compress and convert to MP4.

**Videos**
Resize, compress, convert to GIF, trim a segment and extract the audio as MP3.
